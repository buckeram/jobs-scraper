Agile Software Development Manager, Limerick

Our client is a well established international company in the fast paced international telecoms software sector. They have a large software development centre in Limerick City, and due to continued expansion, they have retained PBRecruitment to hire an Agile Software Development Manager. This is a key role on the senior management team, and a generous remuneration package is on offer for the right candidate.

Job description:

As Software Development Manager you will be reporting into the COO and you will head up the development teams, which are located in Limerick and in the UK, and various outsourced off-shore resources. Leading a large team of developers, you will ensure the best quality software for our customers, on time, to quality, and budget. You will play a leading role in the planning and implementation of the company’s agile transformation. You will provide leadership and guidance to coach, motivate, and lead team members to their optimum performance levels and career development from graduate upwards and you will be part of the senior technology leadership team, advising on technology, business and product strategy.

 

Required skills and experience:

You have a primary degree in a relevant subject area (Electronic Engineering, Computer Science etc) and you have proven success as a software engineering development manager, ideally with experience of managing both on shore and off shore teams. Technically the preferred development background is enterprise scale Java J2EE development. You should have a track record of managing career development of software engineers from graduate intake to consulting professional level.

Experience of leading agile engineering teams, and ideally experience of introducing agile engineering disciplines is important, as is strong leadership, project management, time management and problem solving skills.

Knowledge of any of the following would be desirable: IBM WebSphere, telecoms sector, experience of Continuous Delivery, DevOps and DevOps tooling, database technologies.
